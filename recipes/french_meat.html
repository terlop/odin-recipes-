this is a french meat recipe

The French never heard of it, but the Russians love it.

5 steps

step 1

Lay out the small chunks of meat on a baking tray smeared with vegetable oil.

step 2

Cover the pork evenly with a layer of onion rings.

step 3

Cut the peeled potatoes into thin slices and place them on top of the onions.

step 4

Grate the hard cheese and sprinkle it over the meat. Afterwards, profusely cover it with mayonnaise using a tablespoon or a cooking brush.

step 5

Bake the dish at a temperature of 180-200 degrees Celsius for about 30 minutes. You can tell if the dish is ready by the top layer of cheese. If the crust is golden, then the French-style Meat is ready.

ingredients

  • 500 grams of moderately fat pork
  • 600 grams of potatoes
  • 4 large onions
  • 300 grams of hard cheese
  • 200-250 grams of mayonnaise
  • salt, pepper, herbs and seasoning to taste
